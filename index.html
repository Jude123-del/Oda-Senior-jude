Welcome to Oda Senior High!

Oda Senior High School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing a quality education for students in Accra, Ghana. We offer a rigorous academic program in a supportive and nurturing environment, preparing our students for success in college and beyond.

Academics

Oda Senior High offers a wide range of academic programs, including:

  • General Science
  • Business
  • Arts

  • Information Communication Technology (ICT)

  • Music Club
  • Science Club

Admissions

We welcome applications from all qualified students. To learn more about our admissions process, please visit our website or contact our admissions office.
